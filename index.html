Spaceships Online

A Project by Stefan Kussmaul

Written from scratch in pure Javascript, with Node.js backend

Use 'A' and 'D' to rotate, 'W' to accelerate forward, and 'S' to brake. Use 'Space' to shoot bullets.

This game is in really early stages! Multiplayer is kind of working, you can check it out by opening two windows side-by-side. Note that the different game states won't be in sync! That's next on the to-do list ;)
